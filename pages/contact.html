Common Questions

Frequently Asked

What are your gym timings? +
We're open Monday to Friday from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM, Saturday from 6:00 AM to 9:00 PM, and Sunday from 8:00 AM to 2:00 PM. Check our WhatsApp for holiday schedule updates.
Do you offer a free trial session? +
Yes! We offer a complimentary first consultation and gym tour for all new members. Personal training clients can also book a free 30-minute trial session with one of our trainers. Contact us to schedule.
Is RedRocX suitable for beginners? +
Absolutely! We welcome members of all fitness levels. Our trainers will assess your current fitness level and design a beginner-friendly program that progressively builds your strength and stamina safely.
Do you have special programs for women? +
Yes! We have a dedicated Women's Fitness program, PCOD and hormonal balance nutrition plans, post-natal fitness programs, and a safe, comfortable environment that prioritizes women's wellness at every step.
Can I combine fitness training with nutrition coaching? +
Yes, and we highly recommend it! Our Body Transformation and Elite membership packages combine personalized fitness training with nutrition coaching for the fastest, most sustainable results.
Do you offer corporate wellness programs? +
Yes! We offer customized corporate wellness programs for companies in the Pen and Raigad region, including group sessions, health workshops, and employee fitness packages. Contact us for corporate rates.
