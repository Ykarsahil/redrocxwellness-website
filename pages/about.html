Founder & Director
Tarannum Kazmi
Founder & Head of Wellness
"I started RedRocX with one simple belief — that premium fitness should be accessible to everyone in our community, not just in big cities."

Tarannum Kazmi founded REDROC X Wellness in 2018 with a vision to bring a world-class fitness experience to Pen, Maharashtra. With a background in sports science and holistic wellness, she built a space that addresses not just physical fitness but the complete mind-body-nutrition triad.

Under her leadership, RedRocX has grown into the region's premier wellness destination, serving hundreds of members with specialized programs for women's health, corporate wellness, and lifestyle transformation.
